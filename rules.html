Rules of the Games

Memory

1. Objective

Match all pairs of cards by flipping them two at a time.

2. How to Play

Click on a card to flip it. Click on another card to find a match. If the cards don’t match, they will flip back. Continue until all pairs are found.

3. Game over

The game ends when all pairs of cards are matched.

Run or Die

1. Objective

Run as far as possible while avoiding obstacles to survive.

2. How to Play

Use the mouse to jump. Jump over obstacles.

3. Scoring

Score points based on the distance traveled and obstacles overcome. The further you go, the higher your score.

4. Game Over

The game ends if you collide with an obstacle. Restart the game to try again.

Snake

1. Objective

Eat the food to grow your snake while avoiding collisions with the walls and your own tail.

2. How to Play

Use arrow keys to control the snake's direction. Eat the food to increase your length and score.

3. Scoring

Each piece of food increases your score by 10 points. The longer the snake, the higher the challenge.

4. Game Over

The game ends if the snake hits the wall or its own tail. Restart to try again.
